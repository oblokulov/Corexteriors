Deck Restoration

Professional Deck Restoration in London, Ontario

Is your deck looking worn, grey, or slippery? Over time, weather, UV rays, and foot traffic take their toll on wooden decks. Our professional deck restoration service brings your outdoor space back to life.

Our Deck Restoration Process

1

Inspection & Quote

We assess your deck's condition and provide a detailed, no-obligation quote.

2

Power Washing

We use soft washing techniques to safely remove dirt, mold, mildew, and algae without damaging the wood.

3

Sanding

We sand the surface to remove splinters, old stain, and create a smooth finish.

4

Staining & Sealing

We apply premium oil-based stains with built-in sealers for long-lasting protection.
