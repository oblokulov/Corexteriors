Gutter Cleaning

Professional Gutter Cleaning in London, Ontario

Clogged gutters can cause serious water damage to your home's foundation, siding, and landscaping. In London's rainy climate, regular gutter maintenance is essential to protect your property.

Why Gutter Cleaning Matters

  • Prevent Foundation Damage: Overflowing gutters can erode soil and crack foundations
  • Protect Your Roof: Backed-up water can cause ice dams and roof rot
  • Avoid Basement Flooding: Proper drainage keeps water away from your home
  • Prevent Pest Infestations: Standing water attracts mosquitoes and rodents

Our Gutter Cleaning Process

1

Debris Removal

We remove all leaves, sticks, and debris from your gutters and eavestroughs.

2

Downspout Flushing

We flush all downspouts to ensure proper water flow.

3

Flow Testing

We run water through the system to verify proper drainage.

4

Cleanup

We bag and remove all debris from your property.
