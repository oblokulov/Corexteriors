Hardscape Optimization

Professional Hardscape Optimization in London, Ontario

Over time, interlock patios and driveways can become uneven, develop weeds, and lose their original beauty. Our hardscape optimization service restores the safety and appearance of your outdoor surfaces.

Our Hardscape Services

1

Interlock Leveling

We lift and re-level sunken or uneven pavers to eliminate trip hazards and pooling water.

2

Polymeric Sand

We apply premium polymeric sand between pavers to prevent weed growth and ant infestations.

3

Power Washing

We remove dirt, moss, algae, and stains from your patio, driveway, or walkway.

4

Sealing (Optional)

We apply a protective sealant to enhance color and protect against the elements.
