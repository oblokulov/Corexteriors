Siding Cleaning

Professional Siding Cleaning in London, Ontario

Is your home's exterior looking green, streaky, or dingy? Mold, mildew, algae, and dirt can make your siding look old and neglected. Our professional soft wash technique safely restores your home's curb appeal.

Why Soft Washing?

Unlike high-pressure washing which can damage siding, our soft wash method uses low pressure and biodegradable cleaning solutions to safely remove contaminants. This approach:

  • Won't damage vinyl, aluminum, stucco, brick, or wood siding
  • Kills mold, mildew, and algae at the root for longer-lasting results
  • Uses eco-friendly, biodegradable cleaning agents
  • Safe for your landscaping and plants

Our Process

1

Inspection

We assess your siding type and level of contamination.

2

Pre-Treatment

We apply biodegradable cleaning solution to break down organic growth.

3

Soft Wash

Low-pressure rinse removes all dirt, mold, and cleaning solution.

4

Final Rinse

Complete rinse ensures no residue is left behind.
